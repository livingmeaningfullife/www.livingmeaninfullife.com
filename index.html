Have Any Questions? FAQ's

Find answers to commonly asked questions about Living Meaningful Life

What is the full form of LML?

LML stands for Living Meaningful Life.

Who runs LML?

LML is an e-community of IIT Kharagpur Alumni. So it is run by world-wide alumni community of IIT Kharagpur who have common purpose in living meaningful life.

So many Societies... Is LML just another Society of IIT Kharagpur?

Certainly not. This is e-community of your Alumni who live by what they teach. The unique thing is that you continue to stay on with us till you graduate, after you graduate & forever. Even more uniqueness is in the purpose – our purpose is to help you get real direction along with the speed of technology you learn, so that you start living meaningful life based on eternal principles of Bhagavad Gita.

Will I be charged for the webinar or anything?

Yes. But, not in terms of money. That we, your Alumni, will bare. You need to qualify with your eagerness to really want to be benefitted. Nothing comes free.
